Hi, I'm Chris Ge, a junior majoring in CS (course 6-3) at MIT. My research interests lie in **understanding model behavior**, through a combination of interpretability methods and better analysis of model evaluations.

I'm currently exploring how Diffusion Transformers represent light and other physical properties internally by adapting mechanistic interpretability techniques from the language domain to text-to-image models in [Antonio Torralba Lab](https://groups.csail.mit.edu/vision/torralbalab/). Previously, I did a research fellowship with [Fulcrum](https://fulcrum.inc/), where I developed a method to extract useful features about agentic coding tasks for determining the task's difficulty to an agent. I will be an Algo Developer intern at Hudson River Trading this summer. Besides research, I'm a TA for [Introduction to Machine Learning (6.3900)](https://introml.mit.edu/spring26) at MIT, where I teach a recitation section and help develop course content.
