Hi, I'm Chris Ge, a junior majoring in CS (course 6-3) at MIT. My research interests lie in **understanding model behavior**, through a combination of interpretability methods and better analysis of model evaluations.

I'm currently doing research in [Antonio Torralba Lab](https://groups.csail.mit.edu/vision/torralbalab/) on mechanistically understanding (text+image)-to-image generation from reference images in Diffusion Transformer models like FLUX.2. Previously, I did a research fellowship with [Fulcrum](https://fulcrum.inc/), where I developed a method to extract useful features about agentic coding tasks for determining the task's difficulty to an agent. I will be an Algo Developer intern at Hudson River Trading this summer. Besides research, I'm a TA for [Introduction to Machine Learning (6.3900)](https://introml.mit.edu/spring26) at MIT, where I teach a recitation section and help develop course content.
